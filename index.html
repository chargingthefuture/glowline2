GLOWLINE2

Steer the dart through the maze. Brush the walls to charge your boost.

How to play
  • Steer: Left / Right arrows or A / D. On a phone, the two buttons at bottom-left.
  • Boost: Space or Up, or the button at bottom-right. Needs charge.
  • Charge: Slide along a wall at a shallow angle to fill the boost meter.
  • Gravity levels: The last two mazes pull you down — steer up to hold your line, and grind the roof or floor to keep charging.
  • Restart: R at any time.
  • Sound: M or the speaker button turns sound on or off.
  • Install it: Add it to your home screen (or install from the browser) to play full-screen and offline.
